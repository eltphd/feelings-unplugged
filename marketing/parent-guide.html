Quick crisis checklist

If they mention suicide: Stay calm, thank them, ask directly, remove means, call 988 or text HOME to 741741, stay with them.

If they come out: Affirm immediately, ask how to support, use the name/pronouns they ask for, do your own learning, protect their safety.

Red flags requiring action: Self-harm, explicit plans, prolonged withdrawal, trauma disclosure, substance use to cope.

Cultural considerations

Black families: Validate racial stress, honor code-switching, connect to Black therapists when possible.

Immigrant families: Name the weight of translation labor, honor cultural mixing, listen to the push-pull between home and dominant culture.

Queer + Trans families: Safety planning includes school/community advocacy, pronoun respect, and access to affirming care.
