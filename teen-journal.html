Teen Journal: Feelings Unplugged

Where Your Shadow Meets the Light

Welcome

Your emotions don't fit in boxes. Neither do you.

This journal is for the creators, dreamers, advocates, and truth-tellers navigating identity in a world that often demands you choose between visibility and safety. It's for the ones who refuse to shrink.

What This Journal IS:

  • A safe container for naming emotions without shame
  • A space to explore your identity through 5 Academy archetypes
  • A tool for tracking growth, patterns, and breakthroughs
  • A trauma-informed companion that validates your shadow work

What This Journal IS NOT:

  • Another gratitude journal telling you to "think positive"
  • A replacement for therapy or crisis support
  • A surveillance tool (your thoughts are yours alone)

Your voice is sacred. Your shadow deserves light. Your brilliance is not conditional.

How to Use This Journal

Frequency: Use 1-3 times daily (Morning, Midday, Evening) or whenever emotions feel too big

No rules: Skip prompts that don't resonate. Revisit ones that do. Draw instead of write. Write 10 pages or 10 words. This is your space.

Track patterns: Review weekly to notice emotional trends, triggers, and growth moments

Crisis support: If you're in immediate danger, put down this journal and reach out:

  • 988 Suicide & Crisis Lifeline (call or text)
  • Crisis Text Line: Text HOME to 741741
  • Trevor Project (LGBTQ+ Youth): 1-866-488-7386

What's Inside: 8 Complete Sections

Section 1: Morning Glow - Grounding + Intention Setting

"The best way to start is to start. Even if your light feels dim this morning, it's still there."

  • What energy am I bringing into today?
  • What do I need to feel safe today?
  • Today's intention + affirmation
  • Body check-in + permission slip

Section 2: Midday Spark - Emotional Check-In + Reframing

"How you feel at 2pm might not be how you felt at 8am. That's called being human."

  • Mood check + dominant emotion
  • What triggered this feeling?
  • Reframe + coping strategy
  • Energy inventory + pivot question

Section 3: Evening Calm - Processing + Release + Hope

"The day is done. You made it through. That deserves acknowledgment."

  • How did today actually go?
  • What emotion showed up most?
  • Release ritual + tomorrow's boundary
  • Letter to future self + closing affirmation

Section 4: Academy Identity Archetypes

Discover your firefly type. Every firefly glows differently.

  • The Catalyst: Change-Maker, Disruptor, Truth-Teller
  • The Luminary: Creative, Expressive, Visible
  • The Guardian: Protector, Nurturer, Holder of Space
  • The Maverick: Independent, Unconventional, Freedom-Seeker
  • The Weaver: Connector, Bridge-Builder, Harmony-Seeker

Section 5: Shadow Work Prompts

"Your shadow isn't the enemy. It's the part of you that learned to survive."

  • What emotion am I most afraid of feeling?
  • What part of myself do I hide from others?
  • When do I feel most like an imposter?
  • What coping mechanism isn't serving me anymore?
  • If my shadow could speak, what would it say?

Section 6: Weekly Reflection - Tracking Patterns Over Time

  • Emotional weather report
  • Growth tracker
  • Energy levels (rate each day 1-10)
  • Next week's intention

Section 7: Playlist Therapy - Songs for Every Feeling

Music regulates emotions when words can't. Build your emotional soundtrack.

  • Anger Playlist (songs that let you rage safely)
  • Sad Playlist (songs that let you cry it out)
  • Joy Playlist (songs that make you feel alive)
  • Calm Playlist (songs that ground you)
  • Hype Playlist (songs that remind you you're powerful)
