# Welcome

Your emotions don't fit in boxes. Neither do you.

This journal is for the creators, dreamers, advocates, and truth-tellers navigating identity in a world that often demands you choose between visibility and safety. It's for the ones who refuse to shrink.

**What This Journal IS:**

- A safe container for naming emotions without shame
- A space to explore your identity through 5 Academy archetypes
- A tool for tracking growth, patterns, and breakthroughs
- A trauma-informed companion that validates your shadow work

**What This Journal IS NOT:**

- Another gratitude journal telling you to "think positive"
- A replacement for therapy or crisis support
- A surveillance tool (your thoughts are yours alone)

Your voice is sacred. Your shadow deserves light. Your brilliance is not conditional.

\newpage

# How to Use This Journal

**Frequency:** Use 1-3 times daily (Morning, Midday, Evening) or whenever emotions feel too big

**No rules:** Skip prompts that don't resonate. Revisit ones that do. Draw instead of write. Write 10 pages or 10 words. **This is your space.**

**Track patterns:** Review weekly to notice emotional trends, triggers, and growth moments

**Crisis support:** If you're in immediate danger, put down this journal and reach out:

- **988 Suicide & Crisis Lifeline** (call or text)
- **Crisis Text Line:** Text HOME to 741741
- **Trevor Project (LGBTQ+ Youth):** 1-866-488-7386

# Section 1: Morning Glow

## Grounding + Intention Setting

> *"The best way to start is to start. Even if your light feels dim this morning, it's still there."*

### Morning Prompts

Use these prompts to ground yourself and set intentions for the day ahead:

1. **What energy am I bringing into today?** (Tired? Anxious? Excited? Numb?)

\vspace{2cm}

2. **What do I need to feel safe today?** (Boundaries? Quiet? Connection? Space?)

\vspace{2cm}

3. **One thing I'm grateful for right now** (Even if it's small: clean socks, hot water, your playlist)

\vspace{2cm}

4. **Today's intention:** I will prioritize _______________

\vspace{2cm}

5. **Affirmation:** Write one truth about yourself (Ex: "I deserve rest" / "My anger is valid" / "I'm still learning")

\vspace{2cm}

\newpage

6. **Body check-in:** Where am I holding tension? (Jaw? Shoulders? Stomach?)

\vspace{2cm}

7. **What would make today feel like a win?** (Survival? Joy? Productivity? All are valid)

\vspace{2cm}

8. **Who/what might drain my energy today?** (Name it so you can prepare)

\vspace{2cm}

9. **Song of the day:** What matches your mood or the mood you want?

\vspace{2cm}

10. **Permission slip:** Today, I give myself permission to _______________

\vspace{2cm}

\newpage

# Section 2: Midday Spark

## Emotional Check-In + Reframing

> *"How you feel at 2pm might not be how you felt at 8am. That's called being human."*

### Midday Prompts

Pause in the middle of your day to check in with yourself:

1. **Mood check:** On a scale of 1-10, where am I right now?

\vspace{2cm}

2. **What's the dominant emotion?** (Name it: frustration, overwhelm, contentment, boredom)

\vspace{2cm}

3. **What triggered this feeling?** (Person? Situation? Memory? Thought spiral?)

\vspace{2cm}

4. **Is this emotion trying to tell me something?** (Anger = boundary violation? Sadness = unmet need?)

\vspace{2cm}

5. **Reframe:** What's one other way to see this situation?

\vspace{2cm}

\newpage

6. **Coping strategy:** What would help me regulate right now? (Walk? Music? Text a friend? Breathe?)

\vspace{2cm}

7. **What's in my control vs. out of my control?** (Focus energy on what you can shift)

\vspace{2cm}

8. **Who/what brought me joy today** (Even for 5 seconds)

\vspace{2cm}

9. **Energy inventory:** What % of my energy is left? What needs protecting?

\vspace{2cm}

10. **Pivot question:** If this day could turn around, what would that look like?

\vspace{2cm}

\newpage

# Section 3: Evening Calm

## Processing + Release + Hope

> *"The day is done. You made it through. That deserves acknowledgment."*

### Evening Prompts

Reflect on your day and prepare for rest:

1. **How did today actually go?** (Not how it was "supposed" to go)

\vspace{2cm}

2. **What emotion showed up most today?** (Track this over time for patterns)

\vspace{2cm}

3. **Something I'm proud of** (Surviving hard things counts)

\vspace{2cm}

4. **Something I wish I'd done differently** (No shame, just noticing)

\vspace{2cm}

5. **Release ritual:** What am I letting go of before bed? (Write it, then mentally burn it)

\vspace{2cm}

\newpage

6. **Tomorrow's boundary:** What will I say no to? What will I say yes to?

\vspace{2cm}

7. **Gratitude (realistic version):** What didn't go wrong today?

\vspace{2cm}

8. **Letter to future self:** What does tomorrow's version of me need to know?

\vspace{2cm}

9. **Playlist therapy:** 3 songs that matched today's emotional arc

\vspace{2cm}

10. **Closing affirmation:** I am ___ (Enough? Learning? Resilient? Still here?)

\vspace{2cm}

# Section 4: Academy Identity Archetypes

## Discover Your Firefly Type

Every firefly glows differently. These 5 archetypes help you understand how you show up in the world.

**Instructions:** Read each description. You might resonate with multiple archetypes, or one might feel like home. Use the reflection questions to explore your identity.

\newpage

## The Catalyst

**Core Energy:** Change-Maker, Disruptor, Truth-Teller

**Shadow Side:** Burnout from carrying too much, anger when change is slow

**Affirmation:** *My voice disrupts systems that harm. I don't have to fix everything alone.*

### Reflection Questions:

**When do I feel most powerful?**

\vspace{3cm}

**What injustice am I most passionate about?**

\vspace{3cm}

**How do I protect my energy while advocating?**

\vspace{3cm}

\newpage

## The Luminary

**Core Energy:** Creative, Expressive, Visible

**Shadow Side:** Performing for approval, fear of being "too much"

**Affirmation:** *My light is not conditional on others' comfort. I shine as I am.*

### Reflection Questions:

**When do I dim my light? Why?**

\vspace{3cm}

**What would I create if no one was watching?**

\vspace{3cm}

**How do I define success for myself (not others' definitions)?**

\vspace{3cm}

\newpage

## The Guardian

**Core Energy:** Protector, Nurturer, Holder of Space

**Shadow Side:** Self-sacrifice, neglecting own needs

**Affirmation:** *I can care for others AND myself. My wellbeing matters.*

### Reflection Questions:

**Who do I protect? Who protects me?**

\vspace{3cm}

**When do I give more than I have?**

\vspace{3cm}

**What boundaries would honor my needs?**

\vspace{3cm}

\newpage

## The Maverick

**Core Energy:** Independent, Unconventional, Freedom-Seeker

**Shadow Side:** Isolation, rejecting help even when needed

**Affirmation:** *Independence doesn't mean I have to do it alone. I can be free AND connected.*

### Reflection Questions:

**What rules/expectations do I reject? Why?**

\vspace{3cm}

**When do I push people away?**

\vspace{3cm}

**How can I ask for support without losing myself?**

\vspace{3cm}

\newpage

## The Weaver

**Core Energy:** Connector, Bridge-Builder, Harmony-Seeker

**Shadow Side:** People-pleasing, losing self in relationships

**Affirmation:** *I build bridges without erasing myself. My needs are part of the equation.*

### Reflection Questions:

**Whose peace am I prioritizing over my own?**

\vspace{3cm}

**When do I code-switch or perform?**

\vspace{3cm}

**How do I stay whole while connecting?**

\vspace{3cm}

# Section 5: Shadow Work Prompts

## Facing What's Hard

> *"Your shadow isn't the enemy. It's the part of you that learned to survive."*

These prompts go deeper. Use them when you feel ready to explore the harder emotions:

1. **What emotion am I most afraid of feeling?** (Anger? Sadness? Shame?) Why?

\vspace{3cm}

2. **What part of myself do I hide from others?** What would happen if I let it show?

\vspace{3cm}

3. **When do I feel most like an imposter?** What makes me doubt my worthiness?

\vspace{3cm}

\newpage

4. **What trauma am I still carrying?** (You don't have to process it here—just name that it exists)

\vspace{3cm}

5. **What coping mechanism isn't serving me anymore?** (Numbing? Perfectionism? Isolation?)

\vspace{3cm}

6. **What would I need to believe to feel worthy?** (And why don't I believe it yet?)

\vspace{3cm}

7. **Who hurt me that I haven't forgiven?** (Yourself included)

\vspace{3cm}

\newpage

8. **What grief am I avoiding?** (Loss of person, dream, version of yourself)

\vspace{3cm}

9. **What boundary have I been too afraid to set?**

\vspace{3cm}

10. **If my shadow could speak, what would it say?**

\vspace{3cm}
