## Design System Framework

### Color Psychology for Mental Health
**What to avoid:**
- Overly bright/neon (can trigger anxiety)
- Pure white backgrounds (harsh, clinical)
- Too dark (can feel heavy for depression/anxiety)

**What works:**
- Warm neutrals with grounding earth tones
- Soft, muted colors with adequate contrast
- Colors that feel like a warm hug, not a sterile clinic
